Talking Tree

Gramin Mahavidyalay

Welcome to the Talking Tree app of the Gramin (ACS) Mahavidyalaya, Vasant Nagar, Nanded!


Dr. Sarang Dhote, an assistant professor in the Department of Chemistry at Shri Shivaji Science College in Nagpur, India created the Talking Tree app with the assistance of Mr. SANJAY PATIL, the Head & Assistant Professor Department of Computer Science, Gramin (ACS) Mahavidyalaya. The purpose of this app is to give users a distinctive and interesting experience while learning about the flora of campus of Gramin (ACS) Mahavidyalaya. Visitors can find QR code plates connected to trees using the Talking Tree app. The tree "comes alive" and presents itself to the user by taking their name and providing information about its species, environment, and other fascinating facts by scanning the code with the smartphone. The Talking Tree app's ability to operate in offline mode is one of its primary benefits, allowing users to get information about the trees and their surroundings even when they are not connected to the internet. The app is accessible to a wider audience of users because to its compatibility with both iPhone and Windows phones. The Talking Tree app is the ideal travel companion for your next trip to the ACS Botanical Garden, whether you're a student, a nature enthusiast, or just interested about the trees there. Now that you have it, explore the forest's treasures in a whole new way!
